Projects

Civil Engineering Course Project

At Manhattan College, I worked on a project involving the surveying of a parking lot, designing brick walls for the Higgins Engineering and Science Center, and calculating heat loss for the building. We utilized Electronic Distance Measurement (EDM) instruments for accurate data collection and focused on precise surveying techniques to ensure the quality of our design and analysis.

Transportation Engineering Course Project

Led a team in analyzing traffic patterns at the Mosholu Parkway and Paul Avenue junction in the Bronx, NY. We used Poisson models to predict traffic scenarios and evaluated lane capacity. Our analysis showed that the lane was underutilized even during peak hours, suggesting potential for optimization. This project enhanced my skills in data analysis, traffic engineering, and team leadership.
